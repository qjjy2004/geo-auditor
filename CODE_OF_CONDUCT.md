# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful. Different viewpoints are valuable.
- Constructive criticism is welcome. Personal attacks are not.
- Focus on what's best for the community.

## Enforcement

Report issues to dev@zhibi.xyz. All reports will be reviewed and will result in a response deemed necessary and appropriate.
